I'm strictly a developer at heart. Give me a complex backend problem or a React component to optimize, and I'm happy. But ask me to pick a color palette or design a logo? That's where I usually stare at a blank screen for hours.

I realized I wasn't alone in this. Many of us "indie hackers" and developers have great ideas but get stuck on the visual identity part of launching a product. We just need something "good enough" to get started and look professional.

That is exactly why I built **InitBrand**.

[InitBrand](https://initbrand.dev) is an AI-powered tool designed to generate a complete brand identity for your project in seconds, not days.

## üöÄ What it does

The goal was to make the process as frictionless as possible:

1.  **Describe your project**: Just type a sentence about what you are building.
2.  **AI Magic**: The app uses advanced image generation models (via Replicate) to create unique logo concepts.
3.  **Automatic Branding**: It doesn't just stop at the image. It analyzes the logo to extract a harmonious color palette (Primary, Secondary, Accent) that you can immediately use in your CSS or Tailwind config.
4.  **Download & Go**: You get a nice brand kit zip file with everything you need.
